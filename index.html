Summary & Next Steps

What We Learned

1. T3 effect is detectable in wearables (+22 bpm mean HR rise)
2. Time of day matters - afternoon doses show stronger response
3. Best periods were on 5-6.25 mcg/day total T3 (split 2x or weighted AM)

Limitations

1. Confounding - T4 dose changed alongside T3 changes
2. Adaptation - 6-month stable period may have blunted acute response
3. Coverage - Only 54% of medication days have Whoop HR data

Recommendations

1. Weight AM dose higher - Current 3.75 AM + 2.5 PM appears optimal
2. Monitor afternoon HR - Best signal window for tracking response
3. Track symptoms with journal - Energy, brain fog ratings would strengthen analysis
