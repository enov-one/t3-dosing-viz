Key Finding: Normal CYP Metabolism

All major cytochrome P450 drug-metabolizing enzymes (CYP2D6, CYP2C19, CYP2C9) show normal metabolizer phenotypes. This means standard drug dosing recommendations apply for most medications.

Notable: Rapid CYP1A2 Metabolism

The rs762551 AA genotype indicates rapid CYP1A2 induction. This affects:

  • Caffeine - metabolized quickly, may tolerate higher intake
  • Clozapine - may require higher doses
  • Theophylline - faster clearance
  • Melatonin - may need higher doses for effect

Note: Intermediate COMT Activity

The rs4680 AG (Val/Met) genotype shows intermediate COMT enzyme activity. This affects:

  • Dopamine metabolism - intermediate prefrontal cortex dopamine levels
  • Estrogen metabolism - moderate catechol estrogen clearance
  • Stress response - balanced "warrior" vs "worrier" traits
