Autonomic Concordance Index (ACI)

Novel algorithm: instead of using raw wearable values (which fail due to the inverted-U problem), ACI uses the correlation structure between signals. When the dose is right, sympathetic and parasympathetic signals are concordant. When overdosed, they diverge (RHR drops but HRV also drops, temperature up but sleep quality down).

Method: 7-day rolling Spearman correlation matrix across 5 signals → Frobenius distance from A+ template → normalized ACI score (1 = optimal match). Directional arm uses z-scored skin temp − z-scored RHR to differentiate overdose from underdose.

ACI VALIDATED: A+ protocols have higher concordance (separation = +0.087). A+ protocols average 0.480 ACI vs non-A+ 0.393.
